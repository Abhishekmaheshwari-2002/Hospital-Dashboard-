🏥 Hospital Dashboard – Power BI Project

This Power BI dashboard provides a detailed overview of hospital operations including patient statistics, medicine sales, doctor appointments, and stock levels. It was built using real-world healthcare data and showcases visual insights such as:

  • 💊 Monthly medicine sales and stock status
  • 🧾 Patient billing and charges breakdown
  • 👨‍⚕️ Doctor activity and salary analytics
  • 📊 Department-wise patient analysis
