🏥 Advanced Hospital Analytics Dashboard – Power BI Project

This interactive Power BI dashboard offers a comprehensive analysis of key hospital operations and healthcare metrics. Designed to provide actionable insights for decision-makers, the dashboard consolidates patient data, financial performance, medical inventory, and staff efficiency into one cohesive visual experience.

Key highlights of the dashboard include:

  • 💊 Medicine Sales & Stock Trends: Track sales volume, identify high-demand drugs and monitor real-time stock status across departments.
  • 🧾 Patient Billing & Revenue Insights: Visualise billing categories such as surgery, medication, doctor fees, and discounts to understand revenue flow.
  • 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Performance & Engagement: Analyse doctor availability, patient handling capacity, commission rates, and appointment patterns.
  • 📊 Department & Age Group Analytics: Segment patient data by department, age group, and admission/discharge trends for better resource planning.

Whether you're optimising hospital performance or exploring data visualisation, this project demonstrates how real-world healthcare data can be transformed into strategic business intelligence using Power BI.
